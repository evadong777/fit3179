World Obesity Rate in 2021

This choropleth map displays the world population obesity rate , revealing that:

1. The United States has the highest obesity rate in the population, with an obesity rate of 37.3% and a population of 12,304,494.

2. Saudi Arabia has the second highest obesity rate in the population, with an obesity rate of 35% and a population of 12,264,350 people.

3. Canada has the third highest obesity rate in the population, with an obesity rate of 31.3% and a population of 11,953,470 people.

4. It can be seen from the map that the obesity rate in North American countries is higher than that in Asian countries .
